We're Leap.

Most distribution companies want to be the biggest. We want to be the best. Big tends to follow.

We've earned that confidence. We have years of experience in the OR, in the clinic, in the hospital, and at the manufacturer. We built the company we wished existed.

Two hubs. One mission.

Leap operates out of Dallas and Houston, with internal teams across the south central US and a distributor partner network that reaches the rest of the country. We came together from Leap Surgical and DUB Enterprises with a single goal: build a distribution company that surgeons, hospitals, and manufacturers could actually count on.

Meet the Partners.

Four founders. 75+ years in medical device distribution.

Co-Founder, CEO

Allen Mason

Allen leads Leap as CEO. Years in technical infrastructure before he moved into medical devices, where he watched the big players prioritize their margins over their customers and surgeons. Leap is the answer he built.

Co-Founder, VP of Spine

Jonathan is a career field operator who has carried trays, stood in ORs, and built surgeon relationships firsthand. Co-Founder, VP of Sales
